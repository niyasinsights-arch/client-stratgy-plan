Posting rhythm

At 7 posts/month, aim for roughly 2 posts per week (e.g. Wednesday + Saturday), so each post gets room to breathe. Suggested pattern per fortnight: Reel → Carousel → Reel → Static/Reel.

Note: Support every feed post with 2–3 stories (reshare, poll, Q&A, enquiry sticker). Times here are sensible defaults, not verified best-times — check SVN's own Insights after 2–3 weeks and adjust.
