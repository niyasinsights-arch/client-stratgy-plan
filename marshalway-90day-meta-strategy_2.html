Marshalway — Owning the "Never Iron Again" Category in India

Marshalway is launching India's first premium automatic ironing + drying machine into a market that has never seen the category. This is not a "grow the page" plan — it is a category-creation funnel: make India believe the product exists, prove it works, then convert with payback math and urgency.

Brand: @marshalwayofficial · marshalway.com Stage: Cold-start launch (27 followers) Market: Metro India first Model: D2C website purchase
Working assumptions (confirm before ad launch): Price band ₹19,990–29,990 with EMI available · single hero SKU · Shopify/website checkout live with Pixel + CAPI · ad budget ~₹22,000/month (strategy includes splits for ₹18K and ₹25K). If price or AOV differs materially, budget pacing and CAC targets shift — copy and funnel stay valid.

SECTION 02

Meta Funnel Strategy

Category-creation rule: nobody is searching for an "automatic ironing machine" — demand must be manufactured top-down. The funnel front-loads education and proof, and only asks for the sale once a viewer has seen the machine work at least twice.

StageGoalContent & AdsAudiencePrimary KPI
AWARENESS Make metro India know this machine exists and believe the demo Hero demo Reels, "what is this thing" hooks, monsoon problem content, founder intro · Video Views + Reach campaigns Broad metro 25–45 + premium appliance interest stacks 3-sec views, ThruPlays, CPM, video retention >25%
CONSIDERATION Move "interesting" → "I might actually buy this" Payback math, fabric safety series, objection-killer carousels, creator reviews · Engagement + Traffic + Lead campaigns Video viewers, IG engagers, site visitors CTR >1.2%, landing page views, leads, saves/shares
CONVERSION Purchase on marshalway.com (or WhatsApp-assisted order) Offer + EMI framing, urgency, testimonial stack, demo-to-checkout ads · Sales campaigns, Advantage+ from Month 3 Warm retargeting + 1–3% lookalikes of purchasers/ATC Purchases, CAC, ROAS, ATC→purchase rate
RETENTION Reviews, referrals, UGC, accessory/cross-sell later Onboarding content, care tips, "tag us" prompts, referral incentive · Customer-list campaigns (small, always-on) Purchaser custom audience + WhatsApp list Review rate, UGC volume, referral orders
Funnel logic for a ₹25K considered purchase: expect 7–14 day decision cycles, not impulse buys. The system is built so a person sees: demo (TOFU) → math/objection answer (MOFU) → testimonial + offer (BOFU). Attribution: use 7-day click / 1-day view, and judge Month 1 on engagement cost-efficiency, not ROAS.

Week 4+ always-on		 Sales + Engagement hybrid, frequency-capped Sequenced warm audiences (full stack in Section 06) Objection-sequenced: viewer who didn't click gets the math; visitor who didn't ATC gets fabric-safety; ATC abandoner gets offer + scarcity Each ad answers ONE objection. Show real cycle times honestly (Meta + ASCI safe). Minimum 3 creative variants per ad set; kill anything below benchmark after ₹2,000 spend.

SECTION 07

Influencer & UGC Strategy

An unknown brand selling a never-seen category needs borrowed trust faster than ads alone can build it. Budget here buys credibility, not reach.

Micro Influencers (10K–100K)

8–10 collabs over 90 days
  • Tech/gadget reviewers (Hindi + English) — the "is it a gimmick" validators
  • Working-professional lifestyle creators (corporate vlogs, GRWM-for-office)
  • Home & organisation creators, mom creators (monsoon + uniform angle)
  • Regional layer: 2–3 Malayalam + Tamil/Telugu creators for South metro depth
  • Deal: product + ₹8–25K fee; mandatory honest review + whitelisting rights

UGC Creators

Performance creative pipeline
  • 4–6 UGC creators/month producing ad-native scripts (not posted on their pages)
  • Scripts: istri-horror story, payback math, monsoon test, skeptical-spouse reaction
  • ₹3–6K per video; raw, phone-shot, hook-first — these become C3/C4/C5 ad creative
  • Seed 5 units to early customers like "Rahul" persona in exchange for unboxing rights

Collaboration Ideas

Campaign-level activations
  • #7DaysNoIron Challenge — creators live a week on Marshalway, daily Stories
  • Istri-wala reacts — a real pressing vendor tries the machine (viral-bait, respectful tone)
  • Monsoon Stress Test — soaked shirt to boardroom-ready, timed, multiple creators
  • Dry-cleaning bill reveal — creators total their yearly spend on camera
  • All collabs run as Partnership Ads (boosted from creator handle) for native trust
