  • Project name. The plan uses “the airport-corridor project / [Project Name]”. Confirm the actual project (e.g. Creek Town, Coral Bay, or other) and apply it consistently.
  • Brand details. Founding year and any “trusted since / first / only” claim are placeholders. Confirm before publishing.
  • RERA. Show your project’s RERA registration number where required. The Jul 10 and approvals posts reference it as a placeholder.
  • AI videos. Jul 17 & Aug 19 are AI-generated visualizations — label them clearly as such and mark any render as a render.
  • Facilities. Aug 5, 7 & 21 cover amenities — show only facilities that genuinely exist or are clearly labelled “planned”.
  • Statistics. Drive-times, rental yields, appreciation % and infrastructure are placeholders. Fill only with sourced numbers; label estimates “approx.”; frame appreciation as a projection.
  • Testimonials. Use real, consented buyers (Aug 12 & 14) — or label the reel illustrative. Use a person’s name/face only with written permission.
