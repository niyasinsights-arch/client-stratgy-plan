Every Day, Between Posts

Daily stories + weekly rhythm

Feed posts are 3×/week; stories run daily to keep momentum. A steady rhythm trains the audience to interact and keeps the waitlist warm.

🗓️ Weekly rhythm

  • Mon — value / education post (save-worthy)
  • Wed — people: guide or creator or community
  • Fri — desire / conversion (Wayanad pull or CTA)

🗳️ Daily stories

  • Polls, quizzes, this-or-that, countdowns
  • "Ask a Guide" Q&A box → recycle into reels
  • BTS clips + reshared UGC (with permission)

📌 Highlights

  • Our Story · Wayanad · Guides · Join

Before You Post

Keep it honest

This plan is a scaffold built from brand logic and general travel patterns — not verified ground data. Several posts make claims that only work if they're true. Tick each before it goes live.
