Themes to lean on

  • Salary & income — "how much can you earn," comparisons, extra income
  • EMI & money reality — the emotional, relatable hook
  • Admission — ramps every month, soft → hard
  • Memes & moment — 2 memes + 1 weekly trend post for reach

Meme marketing (2 / month)

  • Fresher salary · interview · sales-target · EMI · office/boss memes
  • Built for shares & tags — top of the reach funnel
  • Keep them relatable, never mean-spirited

Moment marketing (weekly)

  • One trend-tied post a week — hook a sales lesson onto whatever's actually trending
  • IPL / football → teamwork · movies → sales psychology · viral audio → relatable
  • The MOMENT slots (Day 9 / 25 / 17) are placeholders — swap in the live trend
CTA ESCALATION — soft from day one, harder as seats fill
