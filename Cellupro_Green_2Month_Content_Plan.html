BEFORE YOU PUBLISH

⚠ VERIFY THESE — they couldn’t be confirmed from the brand materials, or conflict with the official product PDF

  • Product names. Official docs name the B2B product Ecagro and the skincare range Celluglitz. The plan uses “EcoPulp@CP / Pulp@PC”. Pick the canonical names and apply them consistently.
  • Founder details. “Dr. Reshmy R”, “10+ years of research”, “started at 45”, and “Kerala’s first women-led cellulose plant” could not be verified here. The docs do confirm women-led recognition, Startup India / Kerala Startup Mission (WE Start) support, and a Mavelikara HQ. Confirm the name/title and any “first/only” claim before publishing.
  • Statistics. All impact figures (“1 ton = ___ trees”, deforestation %, carbon/waste numbers) are placeholders. Fill them only with numbers you can trace to a real source; label internal estimates “approx.”
  • Feedstock. The official doc centres on rice straw; the plan also lists bagasse, jackfruit and pineapple. Confirm what’s actually used before stating it as fact.
  • Testimonials. Use a real, consented customer — or frame the testimonial reel transparently as illustrative. Use a brand’s name/logo only with written permission.
