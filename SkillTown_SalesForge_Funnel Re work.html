Sales Forge — 3-Month Plan

Content calendar: 8 videos + 4 carousels per month  ·  Admission push: aggressive videos + static posts, ramping each month

Two engines, kept separate. The Content Calendar builds awareness, sales-career belief, salary/income and EMI-reality through 8 videos + 4 carousels a month. The Admission Push is its own aggressive conversion track — admission videos and static posts that scale up month over month and close hard in Month 3. Run the push posts alongside the calendar (on the in-between days, or as boosted posts).

1 · AWARENESS→

Sales-career belief, salary & income, EMI reality, myths and weekly trend posts — broad reach that pulls cold audiences in.

2 · CONSIDERATION→

Skills, negotiation, roadmap, extra income, academy story & behind-the-scenes — build trust and intent with warm viewers.

3 · CONVERSION

The Admission Push (why join, seats, deadline, FAQ, enrol). Runs throughout and peaks in Month 3.
