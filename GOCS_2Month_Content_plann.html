FIFA MATCH-DAY PLAYBOOK — layer over Month 2 and run through the tournament
PRE-MATCH

Team polls, snack-mood polls, and "who are you watching with?" stories to build anticipation.

DURING

Live story updates, reaction stickers, goal-celebration posts, and snack reminders.

HALF-TIME

Quick prompts: "rate your snack combo," "predict the second half," "what are you eating now?"

POST-MATCH

Memes, fan reactions, best moments, and "match-night MVP" snack content.

10 MATCH-DAY IDEAS
Kickoff snack poll. "What are you munching before kickoff?"
Team vs snack pairing. "Which GOCS snack matches your team?"
Half-time crunch reel. GOCS during the 15-minute break.
Goal celebration story. A crunchy reaction clip per goal.
Match-night starter pack. Friends, TV, GOCS, countdown sticker.
Predict the score. Commenters get featured.
Snack captain of the match. Fans vote the best variety.
Watch-party UGC. Fans tag GOCS in their setup.
"What's in your half-time plate?" Story prompt with reposts.
Final whistle recap. "Win or lose, GOCS wins the night."

Your 30-post plan, split evenly across two months — 15 posts each, posting every 2nd day with a rest day between. Month 1 builds familiarity and the first emotional pull; Month 2 deepens the home/nostalgia hooks and climaxes on the match-night finale, so schedule it to line up with the live tournament window. In the calendars, orange = Reel, teal = Story, pink = Carousel; each grid starts on a Sunday, so shift it to match your real start date. Lead with feeling and introduce product (Kozhi Ada, the ₹599 Sampler) only after the emotional groundwork lands.
